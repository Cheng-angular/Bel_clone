Fitness Center and More

at Bel Air Bubble Resort hotel

The fitness center is located on the second floor of the clubhouse Bel Air resort hotel Tremblant. Bel Air fitness center offers the latest equipment from cardio equipment to free weights. Enjoy incredible views of the surrounding mountains as you workout. Nest to the gym is studio with step benches for workouts and matts for yoga and stretching. After a workout you can unwind, relax and detoxify in the sauna. Finish up your routine with a refreshing shower in the womens’ or mens’ locker room.
