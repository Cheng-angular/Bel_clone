property description:

These dome rentals are located just steps from the clubhouse at Bel Air bubble hotel in Tremblant. These modern geodesic shaped domes offer a truly unique experience and the perfect mixture of luxury and nature.

The comfortable open-spaced interior has a king-size bed that faces a large bay opening with spectacular views of the wonderful nature outdoors. At the press of a button a flat screen TV pops up at the foot of the bed.

A cozy leather reclining chair sits at each side of the bed and there is a gas fireplace to keep you warm while you enjoy romantic moments.

The open kitchenette is equipped with everything you may need to prepare a simple meal and there is a dining area that that seats two.

These dome rentals in Mont Tremblant have a modern glass shower with rainfall shower-head and a separate enclosed toilet with a vanity sink.

Outside on the deck there is a BBQ grill and a table and chairs ideal for dining under the stars. You can also relax in your own private hot tub or the sauna which is shared with one another dome.

All dome rentals include free Wi-Fi, Netflix and parking for two cars.
